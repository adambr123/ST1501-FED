Sustainable Campus

  • SP has developed a Sustainable Campus framework to guide its efforts in reducing environmental impact and increasing sustainability.
  • This includes measures such as increasing energy efficiency
  • reducing water usage
  • promoting recycling and waste reduction

Green Spaces

  • SP has created green spaces on campus, including rooftop gardens and green walls
  • to help absorb carbon dioxide and improve air quality.

Sustainable Transport

  • SP has implemented a number of measures to promote sustainable transport on campus
  • such as encouraging the use of bicycles
  • providing charging stations for electric vehicles.

Energy Conservation

  • SP has implemented energy conservation measures across campus
  • this includes the use of energy-efficient lighting
  • air conditioning systems
  • and renewable energy sources such as solar panels.

Recycling and Waste Reduction

  • SP has implemented a comprehensive waste management system
  • It includes the separation of recyclable materials, composting, and the use of biodegradable products.

SP's Common Core Modules

  • There are core modules in every diploma where it is compulsory for students to take these modules.
  • These modules teach students about different global issues
  • expose them to many experiences in the world
  • for example, students will explore the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals where they learn more about the 17 goals each comprising of different world issues.
  • Students from this module will get to grow as a person as they are able to gain perspectives of other countries and understand their needs which allows students to be appreciative of what they have in Singapore.
