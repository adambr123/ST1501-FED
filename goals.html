Opinions and Goals towards Sustainability

Have a recyclable bin in the house

  • Split into different components
  • Convenient to sort them out
  • After accumulating plastic bottles/metal cans, go down to the nearest recycling machine to exchange for some money
  • One example: the government implemented a vending machine to give a recycling box to every household which they can collect it from the machine by using their NRIC
  • This recycling box is also very beneficial as it tells us what items are able to be recycled, educating the people on recycling

Reduce the use of plastic bags by always carrying a recyclable bag and container

  • Reduce the use of takeaway boxes
  • Lead to a decrease in pollution
  • Reduce waste
  • Reduce the need to mass produce these types of paper takeaway boxes.
  • Decreases the need for additional plastic manufacturing
  • Leads to an increase in the use of other, safer materials for the environment.

Make it a point to educate your family and friends about the ways and benefits of recycling

  • Some of them may not know about the benefits of sustainable living
  • By educating them, they would be able to understand how their actions could affect the environment
  • Allow them to take small steps towards sustainable living
  • Promotes environmental sustainability to all

My Opinions

Use natural light when it's during the day

  • Save electricity consumption
  • Lower your carbon footprint
  • Save money and allows you to use your electricity consumption wisely, like on other important electrical appliances.

Minimize the amount of water that you use daily

  • When showering you can limit your shower time to less than 10min in order to save water
  • When raining, you can also use buckets to collect the rainwater which you could use for different aspects and needs in your life.
  • Always keep the tap off when not in use to minimize water wastage

Use electrical appliances like Aircon, washing machine, and fans etc. that have 5 ticks sticker

  • Shows that these appliances are more energy-efficient when using them
  • Fewer greenhouse gases being produced so the earth not retain that much heat in the atmosphere.
  • Reduce electricity
